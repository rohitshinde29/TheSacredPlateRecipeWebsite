IDLI-SAMBAR RECIPE





Ingredients :


    For Soft idli Batter:
  1. dal ½ cup
  2. Ukhda chawal idli rice 1.5 cups
  3. Methi seeds ½ tsp
  4. Salt to taste
    For Sambar:
  1. For sambar masala paste:
  2. Refined oil / coconut oil 2 tbsp
  3. Hing a small piece
  4. Mustard seeds 1 tsp
  5. Cumin seeds 1 tsp
  6. Urad dal 1 tbsp
  7. Chana dal 1 tbsp
  8. Black peppercorns 1 tsp
  9. Methi seeds 1 tsp
  10. Coriander seeds 2 tbsp
  11. Kadi patta 12-14 nos.
  12. Kashmiri red chillies 10-12 nos.
  13. Fresh coconut 1/4th cup (grated)
  14. Garlic 2-3 cloves
  15. Ginger 1 inch
  16. Shallots 4 nos.
  17. Green chillies 1-2 nos. (only if you want it spicy)
  18. Tur/arhar dal 1/3rd cup (washed & soaked well)
  19. Turmeric powder 1/4th tsp
  20. A pinch of salt
  21. Oil 1 tbsp
  22. Mustard seeds ½ tsp
  23. Cumin seeds ½ tsp
  24. Onion 2 medium sized (diced)
  25. Tomatoes 2 medium sized (diced)
  26. Turmeric powder 1 tsp
  27. Kashmiri red chilli powder 1 tsp
  28. Veggies:
  29. Drumstick 1 no. (cut into pieces)
  30. Pumpkin 1/3rd cup (cubed)
  31. Ash gourd 1/3rd cup (cubed)
  32. Brinjal 1 no. (cut into quarters)
  33. Bottle gourd 1/3rd cup (cubed)
  34. Shallots 1/4th cup (sliced)
  35. Jaggery 5-6 tbsp (you can adjust as per your preference)
  36. Tamarind water of a lemon sized ball of tamarind soaked in water
  37. Oil 1 tbsp
  38. Lady finger 5-6 nos.
  39. For tadka
  40. Oil 1 tbsp
  41. Chana dal 1/4th tsp
  42. Methi seeds 1/4th tsp
  43. Mustard seeds 1/4th tsp
  44. Boriya red chillies 4 nos.
  45. Kadi patta 8-10 nos.
  46. Hing 1/4th tsp

Method


Take rice into a bowl and wash it really good with water for 2-3 times. Add water and soak the rice for 4 hours. Take urad dal and add methi seeds. Mix well and wash them really good with water 2-3 times. Add water and soak for 4 hours. Take pohe and wash with water. Soak for just about 5 minutes. Take the dal out into a blender pot and add water. Blend well into fine paste. Transfer the batter into a pot. Take rice and pohe in a blender pot and blend into a little coarse batter. Take the rice batter out into the dal batter. Take a whisk and mix well for about 4-5 minutes. Cover and ferment the batter for 10-12 hours. Add salt and mix well. Take the idly stand and grease it well with oil. Add the batter in each mould and leave some space for idly to puff up. Arrange the idly in crisscross pattern. It means idly in upper dish should come in between 2 lower idlies. Add 1”water in idly cooker and put the idly pot in it. Steam the idlies on medium heat for 20 minutes. Let it rest for 5 minutes more. Take the idly pot out of idly cooker and rest for 5 minutes more. Take the idlies out. Idlies are already. Serve with hot sambar and chutney.
