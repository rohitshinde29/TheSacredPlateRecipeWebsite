Hello fellas! We foodies, welcome you to "The Sacred Plate" recipe blog. We decided to create this blog to share our love,passion & enthusiasm about food to the amazing people who share the same passion towards food. We also want to encourage people to prepare their own food without any fear or hesitation of "How the food will taste after making it." & all the things which prevent them to make the food. Don't worry, the recipes in this blog are less complex and easy to make, so, just enjoy the art of making food & help us to spread the love for food all over the world!
